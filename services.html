Door-to-Door Delivery

Our Door-to-Door service is the epitome of convenience for our clients. We handle the entire logistics process, eliminating the need for you to arrange transport to or from terminals.

  • Pickup: We collect goods directly from your factory, warehouse, or home.
  • Documentation: We handle all waybills, insurance, and compliance paperwork.
  • Customs Clearance: For international shipments, we manage all customs formalities.
  • Final Delivery: Goods are delivered safely to the receiver's specific location.

This service is ideal for e-commerce businesses and individuals looking for a hassle-free shipping experience.

Warehousing Solutions

State-of-the-art storage for your valuable inventory. Our warehouses are strategically located to ensure quick distribution.

  • Inventory Management: Advanced WMS (Warehouse Management Software) to track stock levels in real-time.
  • Security: 24/7 CCTV surveillance, fire safety systems, and guarded access.
  • Value Added Services: Packaging, labeling, kitting, and bundling services available onsite.
  • Scalability: Flexible storage space that grows with your business needs during peak seasons.

Supply Chain Management

We don't just move goods; we optimize your business. Our SCM experts analyze your current logistics flow to identify bottlenecks and cost-saving opportunities.

  • Planning & Strategy: Demand forecasting and route optimization.
  • Sourcing: Assistance with procurement logistics.
  • Visibility: End-to-end visibility of the supply chain through our digital dashboard.
  • Sustainability: Green logistics solutions to reduce carbon footprint.

Express Delivery

When time is money, choose Express. Designed for high-priority shipments that need to reach their destination yesterday.

  • Next-Day Delivery: Available for select domestic locations.
  • Priority Handling: Your shipment skips the queue at sorting centers.
  • Air Freight Integration: Leveraging air cargo for long-distance rapid transit.
  • Live Monitoring: Dedicated support team monitoring your parcel until delivery.

Full Truck Load (FTL)

Dedicated capacity for your bulk shipments. FTL is the best choice when you have enough goods to fill a truck, or when you want a dedicated vehicle for security.

  • Faster Transit: The truck goes directly from Point A to Point B with no stopovers for other pickups.
  • Reduced Damage Risk: No handling of goods at transshipment hubs.
  • Vehicle Variety: From 14ft to 32ft containers and open trailers.
  • GPS Tracking: Track the vehicle location in real-time.

Part Load / LTL

Economic shipping for smaller loads. You don't need to hire a full truck if you don't have enough goods. Pay only for the space you use.

  • Cost Efficiency: Split the transportation cost with other shippers.
  • Regular Connectivity: Daily services to major cities across the country.
  • Consolidation: We professionally consolidate loads to ensure maximum safety.
  • Small & Medium Business: Perfect for SMEs requiring frequent, smaller shipments.
