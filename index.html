Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our logistics services.

How do I track my shipment?

Simply enter your AWB number on our tracking page. Our system provides real-time GPS updates, so you always know where your package is.

Is my cargo insured during transit?

Yes, we offer comprehensive transit insurance. This covers damages due to accidents, theft, or natural calamities ensuring 100% safety of value.

What is the difference between FTL & PTL?

FTL (Full Truck Load) is a dedicated truck for your goods (faster). PTL (Part Truck Load) allows you to share truck space and split costs (economical).

Do you handle international customs?

Absolutely. Our in-house legal team manages all customs documentation, duties, and clearance processes for smooth cross-border shipping.

Can I schedule a pickup for a specific time?

Yes, our 'Scheduled Pickup' service allows you to choose a convenient time slot. Our team will arrive at your doorstep exactly when needed.
