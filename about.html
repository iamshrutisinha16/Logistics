ABOUT US

We Move Your Business
Forward.

Welcome to Sumit Trans Logistics (STL). We are a premier logistics solution provider based in Pune, dedicated to transporting goods safely and efficiently across the nation.

Founded by Mr. Kuldeep Singh, STL was built on a simple promise: "Any load, Any where, On Time." From heavy industrial machinery (Full Truck Load) to smaller commercial parcels (Part Load), we handle it all with professional expertise.

"Our trucks don't just carry goods; they carry the trust of our clients. Safety and timing are our non-negotiable pillars."

- Kuldeep Singh Proprietor, STL
